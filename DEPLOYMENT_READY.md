## 🚀 Next Steps: Deploy to Vercel

### Quick Deploy (5 minutes)

1. **Go to Vercel:**
   - Visit: https://vercel.com/
   - Sign in with GitHub

2. **Import Project:**
   - Click "Add New..." → "Project"
   - Select: `sibasishmisra/classmate`
   - Click "Import"

3. **Add Environment Variable:**
   ```
   CLAUDE_API_KEY=your_api_key_from_env_local
   ```
   ⚠️ Copy the key from your `.env.local` file

4. **Deploy:**
   - Click "Deploy"
   - Wait 2-3 minutes
   - Done! 🎉

### Your App Will Be Live At:
`https://classmate-[random].vercel.app`
